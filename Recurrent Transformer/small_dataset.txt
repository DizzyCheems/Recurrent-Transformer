Hotel Check-in Policy:
Check-in time starts from 3:00 PM. Early check-in is available upon request, subject to availability.

Hotel Check-out Policy:
Check-out time is before 11:00 AM. Late check-out may be allowed with prior approval and may incur additional charges.

Cancellation Policy:
Free cancellation is available up to 24 hours before check-in. Cancellations made within 24 hours of check-in will incur a one-night charge.

Pet Policy:
Pets are allowed at an additional charge of $25 per night. Only small dogs are permitted, and they must remain on a leash in public areas.

Smoking Policy:
This is a non-smoking hotel. Smoking is not permitted in rooms or indoor public spaces. A $200 cleaning fee will be charged for smoking violations.

Payment Policy: 
We accept major credit cards, debit cards, and cash payments. Full payment is required at check-in.

Parking Policy:
Complimentary parking is available for registered guests. Additional parking for visitors is available for $10 per day.

Noise Policy:
Guests are asked to keep noise to a minimum between 10:00 PM and 7:00 AM to ensure a peaceful environment for all.
