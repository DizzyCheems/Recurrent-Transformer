How many bones are in the human skull?
The human skull is made up of 22 bones, which can be categorized into two main sections: the cranium and the facial bones. The cranium houses and protects the brain, while the facial bones give structure to the face and support the senses. These bones are joined together by sutures, which are immovable joints that fuse as a person ages.
